Problem We Solved

1 in 3 women experience physical abuse

1 in 3 of 140 million women in Indonesia experience physical abuse, often tied to financial abuse that leaves them dependent and vulnerable. Without stable jobs or skills, they struggle to break free from this cycle.

Industry Talent Shortage

Over 150,000 beauty salons in Indonesia struggle to find skilled professionals due to a lack of training and connection platforms.

Our Solution

SalonKita empowers women with training and connects them to employers, addressing both unemployment and industry talent shortages. We help women gain financial independence while solving the beauty industry's skilled professional shortage.

Why do we start with beauty?

High Demand and Trends

The beauty industry is experiencing unprecedented growth with increasing demand for skilled professionals across Indonesia.

Express Lane in Generating Income

Beauty services provide immediate income opportunities with flexible working arrangements and entrepreneurial potential.

Based on Research

Our approach is backed by extensive research on women's economic empowerment and industry needs analysis.

Have 10+ Years Experience

Our team brings over a decade of combined experience in beauty industry training and women's empowerment programs.
